# Live Voice Feedback

This document captures example live voice feedback lines for AimMod's in-run `livefeedback`.

## Tone

Voice feedback should be:

- short
- calm
- neutral
- constructive
- lightly positive when deserved
- useful in the next few seconds of gameplay

It should not sound overly dramatic, negative, or analytical.

## Good Style

- "Your accuracy is dropping."
- "Your accuracy is improving."
- "You're on personal best pace."
- "Your aim is smooth. Keep going."

## Avoid

- long explanations during a run
- vague hype
- aggressive criticism
- overly technical phrases
- talking too often

## Example Lines

### Accuracy

- Your accuracy is dropping.
- Your accuracy is improving.
- Your accuracy is stabilizing.
- Your shots are landing more consistently.
- Your hit rate is falling.
- Your hit rate is improving.
- You're taking cleaner shots now.

### Pace

- Your pace is increasing.
- Your pace is slowing down.
- Your pace is stable.
- Your speed is rising.
- Your speed is getting less controlled.
- Your pace is recovering.

### Smoothness And Control

- Your aim is smooth. Keep going.
- Your movement is getting cleaner.
- Your movement is getting shaky.
- Your control is improving.
- Your control is slipping.
- Your corrections are increasing.
- Your hand is more stable now.

### Tracking

- Your tracking is improving.
- Your tracking is smoother now.
- You're staying on target longer.
- Your target hold is improving.
- Your tracking is getting less stable.
- You're fighting the target more now.

### Static Clicking

- Your stops are cleaner.
- Your shot timing is improving.
- Your confirmation is improving.
- You're rushing the shot.
- Your clicks are getting cleaner.

### Dynamic Clicking

- Your timing is improving.
- You're reading the movement better.
- Your shot timing is early.
- Your shot timing is late.
- Your control is holding on moving targets.

### Target Switching

- Your transitions are getting faster.
- Your transitions are slowing down.
- Your target handoff is improving.
- You're staying too long on targets.
- Your switching pace is strong.

### Personal Best And Run Progress

- You're on personal best pace.
- You're close to your personal best.
- You're about to beat your personal best.
- This run is ahead of your average.
- This run is below your average pace.
- You're finishing stronger than usual.
- You're back on pace.

### Recovery And Reinforcement

- Good recovery.
- That adjustment helped.
- That was cleaner.
- You're stabilizing the run.
- Keep this rhythm.
- Nice adjustment.
- This segment is stronger.

## Suggested Trigger Categories

- trend down
- trend up
- recovery
- stability
- pb pace
- closing strong

## Usage Notes

- PB lines should be rare.
- Reinforcement should be used after meaningful recovery or stabilization.
- Negative trends should be stated plainly, without sounding punitive.
- The system should prefer silence over low-value chatter.
- Most lines should help the player adjust during the next 2 to 5 seconds of the run.
